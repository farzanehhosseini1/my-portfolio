About Me

Hello! My name is Farzaneh Hosseini, and I am a passionate graphic designer and digital artist dedicated to creating stunning designs and high-quality assets for the creative industry. With years of experience in graphic design and expertise in advanced software tools, I specialize in producing alpha images and materials tailored for 3D artists, game designers, and visual effects professionals. I take pride in my ability to blend technical precision with artistic creativity, using tools such as Substance Painter, Substance Designer, ZBrush, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Blender. My goal is to craft resources that empower artists to bring their visions to life with ease and precision. Art is not just a profession for me—it’s my passion and calling. I strive to continuously learn, grow, and contribute to the artistic community, pushing the boundaries of creativity with every project.
